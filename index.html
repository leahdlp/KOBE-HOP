WHAT TO DO

This is an infinitely running game. The goal is to get as much points as possible. You get points with each platform that contributes to your vertical progression. Along the way you will encounter defenders trying to prevent you from getting to the hoop! The only way to come in contact with them and not lose the game is to bounce on top of them. Whenever you see a hoop in the game, press spacebar to shoot your ball into it. Once the hoop is on the screen you will have a shot clock. If you make your ball in the hoop, you get bonus points. If you do not shoot by the end of the shot clock, you lose a ball. If you run out of all your balls (you start with 3), you lose. If you fall off the screen, you lose.

DIRECTIONS

Use ARROW KEYS to move LEFT and RIGHT

press SPACEBAR to SHOOT
